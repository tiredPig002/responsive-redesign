10 Questions to Evalutate "Do I Need a Hearing Aid?"

Hearing loss for many people is gradual, so it may often go unnoticed. We have been helping the hearing impaired for over 30 years, so take our 10 question test for clues to find out whether or not you or someone you know needs a hearing aid.

This self-check for your hearing could help you evaluate your condition, or that of an adult loved one. As you view each question, keep track of the number of "yes" answers.

Self-Check List of Questions

  1. Do you find yourself asking people to speak up or repeat themselves?
  2. Do you sometimes feel that people are mumbling or not speaking clearly?
  3. Do you experience difficulty understanding soft or whispered speech?
  4. Do you sometimes have difficulty understanding speech on the telephone?
  5. Do you find it difficult to follow a conversation in a hoisy or crowded room?
  6. Do you sometimes find it difficult to understand a speaker at a meeting?
  7. Does a hearing problem cause you to feel embarassed when meeting new people?
  8. Does a hearing problem cause you to be nervous?
  9. Does a hearing problem cause you to visit people less often than you would like?
  10. Do you find men's voices easier to understand than women's?

Evaluating Your Answers

Did some of the questions surprise you? Often some will especially if you need help.

Yes answers to any of these 10 questions may mean that you have identified a hearing problem. Yes answers to several questions strongly suggests that a hearing check is necessary.
